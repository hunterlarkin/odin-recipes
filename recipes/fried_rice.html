Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a versatile and delicious dish that brings together savory flavors and a delightful mix of ingredients. To make fried rice, start by cooking long-grain rice until tender and then allowing it to cool. In a hot skillet or wok, sauté finely chopped onions, garlic, and vegetables of your choice in a blend of sesame oil and soy sauce. Push the veggies to the side and scramble a couple of eggs in the empty space. Once the eggs are cooked, add the cooled rice to the pan and toss everything together, ensuring the rice gets coated with the flavorful seasonings. Finally, sprinkle with green onions and a dash of additional soy sauce, and you're ready to enjoy a satisfying and comforting plate of homemade fried rice. Feel free to customize your recipe by adding protein such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu, or by incorporating other favorite ingredients like peas, carrots, or diced ham.

ingredients

  • Cooked long-grain rice (preferably cooled)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • Assorted vegetables (such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, and corn)
  • Eggs (scrambled)
  • Green onions (sliced, for garnish)
  • Soy sauce (to taste)
  • Optional proteins (such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
  2. Sauté the Aromatics and Vegetables
  3. Cook the Eggs and Add Rice
  4. Season with Soy Sauce
  5. Garnish and Serve
