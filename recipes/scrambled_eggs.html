Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a simple and popular breakfast dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them until they are softly set and slightly creamy. The eggs are beaten in a bowl with a fork or whisk, seasoned with salt and pepper, and then cooked in a pan with butter or oil over medium-low heat. As they cook, they are gently stirred or folded to create fluffy, curds of cooked egg. Scrambled eggs can be enjoyed on their own, paired with toast or a side of bacon, or used as a base for various additions like cheese, vegetables, or herbs for additional flavor variations.

ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper
  2. Heat butter/oil in a pan
  3. Cook eggs, stirring occasionally
  4. Remove from heat when set
  5. Serve and enjoy delicious eggs
